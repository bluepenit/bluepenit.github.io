Lytic Health

Web and mobile apps for patients and doctors to automatically determine preliminary diagnosis and schedule the appointments.

Main Idea

Doctors spend lots of time on patients that don't have symptoms requiring medical treatment. So, they could have spent that time on people that are really in need of such. The idea is to automate that process and free up the doctors' time.

Mobile app

Mobile app

Home

Home screen comprises general navigation, symptom search first step, saved searches and appointments pending doctor's approval.

Search

The screen includes suggested search functionality along with tagging, that allows having multiple symptoms and more exact diagnosis as a result.

Chat Bot

Bot asks a number of question based on the symptoms chosen on the previous step. Typical messenger like view should give a comfortable impression of talking to a human but not machine.

Appointment

Bot can suggest making an appointment with doctor in case there are health threatening situation. Scheduling process requires just 3 clicks to make that happen.

Web Application

Web Application

Dashboard

Clean summary of most relevant information. Quick access to chat and patient statistics.

Patient List

Table view of all the Patients with their recent statuses. Ability to sort and search is taken into account.

Patient Profile

The most important part of the doctor app. Keeps doctor in the loop of the critical patient data. Colors help to attract the attention to places requiring some action.
