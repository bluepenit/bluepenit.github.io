  • Project discovery

    We are invested in your product’s success  just as much as you are. This is why some of our services concern aspects other than development.


    Before starting design, we’ll help you refine the idea for a diversified eCommerce platform to make it commercially viable. To do that, we’ll analyze your competitors. You’ll know their strengths which you can integrate into the platform. Faults, too — we’ll tell you what to avoid to outshine your competition.


    Aside from that, we’ll provide you with a mind map. You’ll see for yourself how your killer feature can be elevated by other features — and you’ll know exactly how the eCommerce platform will work...

  • UI/UX design

    We’ll make the visual look of your eCommerce app from scratch — app’s logic and branding included.


    We’ll help outline the app’s logic — and create a mind map accordingly. With it, you can see every choice a user can make within the app at a wider scope. For example, how the user navigates through the eCommerce platform and adds items to the shopping cart.


    After you approve the mind map, we use it to create wireframes. They’re essentially barebones versions of the platform’s features. You can check out where the user will access future product cards, place orders, or contact support.


    When that’s settled, we pick out 1-2 wireframes and finalize them in line with your branding strategy. You’ll be able to see the perfected design and provide suggestions if something feels amiss. After we complete every single screen, our designers prepare a UI kit — a bundle of icons and buttons which make development simpler.

  • Development

    We’re a full cycle development company, so you can rest easy knowing you’ll get a fully functioning product from scratch.


    We work primarily with React Native and React.js. This ensures your eCommerce platform works on any device — and your users get a seamless experience: switching from a laptop to browsing your shop on a phone.


    We’ll be with you every step of the way: from developing your eCommerce idea to releasing your platform on mobile app stores.


    After you launch your eCommerce platform, we’ll help make it even greater. If you want to integrate a new feature into your shop, we’ll accommodate. And if there are any malfunctions, our eCommerce developers will get rid of them — and make sure they don’t reappear.

  • Content marketing

    We’ll create compelling copy and help integrate some digital marketing tools to boost your sales.


    Developing a worthwhile platform is half the trouble. To make sure your users stay and keep buying from your platform, you need content that drives customer engagement.


    Our team can help with digital marketing — by writing intriguing copy. With it, you can attract more customers to your platform. From fun pop-ups and buttons to helpful onboarding tips, your users won’t leave the platform prematurely. Instead, they’ll share it with their colleagues and friends — and you’ll get even more customers.


    Thanks to worthwhile content and good digital marketing techniques, you’ll increase customer retention and promote your different sales channels.

  • Consulting

    Thanks to our experience with eCommerce development, we can guide you to create a platform with a great product-market fit. We’ll happily share our knowledge and expertise to give you a competitive advantage.


    Want to integrate a potential new feature? We’ll tell you whether it’s a good idea. Want to use a different monetization model? Our project managers will say whether there are any fallbacks to it — and what to choose instead if there are pitfalls. You’ll get advice that saves money and time — and we have a sizable portfolio to back it up.

  • Prototyping

    We’ll give you access to a prototype before a full-on release. You’ll get to see how your eCommerce platform works in action. This way, you’ll know just how sound your “killer feature” is — and whether it’s enough to attract a solid customer base.


    At the same time, you’ll be able to provide your own feedback: what features to keep and what should work differently. Either way, we’ll listen to your suggestions — and implement them to create a successful eCommerce platform.

Our eCommerce development process: 9 steps

Over the course of many years, we’ve streamlined our process into tangible steps.
If you’re going to partner up with us, you’ll know exactly how it goes — we’re going
to give you updates constantly. You won’t get left behind.


Here are these 9 steps of our eCommerce development services.

  • Project kick-off
    The moment you want to contact us, our project manager gets in touch with you to book a call. That’s when they inquire more about specifics: what’s the business idea of your eCommerce platform, its major selling point, and general requirements. Then, we return with a proper time estimate and an approximate budget — you can prepare accordingly.
  • Project planning
    This is the part when a project manager steps in. They make sure the development goes according to plan and timeframe — to do so, they divide it into 2 week sprints. The manager also assembles a team responsible for your eCommerce platform — they choose our in-house designers and developers with appropriate experience. This way, the overall development process goes smoothly and your platform “hits the shelves” on schedule.
  • UX design

    The first thing we do is market research. We scrutinize your competition to see what can be useful for your product. That includes taking in both their best practices and pitfalls. Thanks to this, your eCommerce platform is sure to outclass other businesses in your niche — by having none of the mistakes they made.


    Then we move on to wireframes — with them, we can predict how your future clients will use your eCommerce platform. We do this in Figma — you can tell us your suggestions right there.

  • UI design

    Our team creates a UI concept — you’ll see how certain features and the interface of your eCommerce platform are going to look like. This is the stage where we fine tune some of the visual aspects of design or branding: fonts and colors. In turn, this makes the user experience all the more pleasant and exciting for the user.


    After the design is approved and finalized, our specialists create the UI kit — a Figma file with standard styles, headings, icons and other elements. This speeds up the process significantly for both designers and developers: it’s easier to code and add features with new screens.

  • Development
    We’re no strangers to SCRUM and Agile methodologies — we regularly use them in our business processes to make sure your eCommerce platform is released on time. Throughout our partnership, your assigned project manager will keep you in the loop on all the new features we managed to add to your product. And at the end of each
    2-week sprint, we’ll share a watered-down demo
    with some of the features with you — you’ll get to test
    it from any device firsthand.
  • QA testing
    We go through all the routine checks and tests while developing your eCommerce platform. It’s an important step — it ensures that no bug or other malfunction seeps into the final version of the app. Your users won’t be annoyed by needless interruptions — they’ll get to buy more products and use your platform more.
  • Preparing for release
    After some finishing touches, we prepare your platform for deployment on all major devices. Whether it’s desktop or mobile — App Store, Google Play Market — we’ll help you launch your eCommerce platform. That’s because we know what to add so your eCommerce platform stays on top of the search results — and works smoothly no matter the browser or OS.
  • Rolling out the platform
    Release doesn’t mark the end of development. This is the part which can help your eCommerce platform stand out from the rest. We’ll look through the reviews of your customers and integrate some of the best ideas into your platform. This way, it’ll attract even more customers who’ll get to buy or sell their products on your eCommerce platform.
  • Support and maintenance
    After all is said and done, we’ll make sure your eCommerce platform stays on top — by incorporating new features and fixing technical challenges. And if you’re planning to scale your business — for example, to pursue a global market with your eCommerce product — we’ll assist you.
