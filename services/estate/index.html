Real estate app development

We develop real estate apps to automate and streamline the process of searching, moving in and house management, including payments for rent and utilities.

Main features

These features will replace letting agents, property managers and other middle-men between tenants, property owners and utility companies:

  • Meter readings collection

  • Advanced search for the right apartments

  • Arrange meetings and polls for tenants

  • Live-chat with tenants, chat-bot for consulting

  • Add card for automated payments

  • Push notification

  • Send customized letters and invoices to tenants

  • Integration with CRM and accounting systems

  • Dashboard for statistics, export of reports, references, etc.

  • Cloud and offline storage for documents
