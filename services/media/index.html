  • How much does it cost to develop a Media & Entertainment app?

    Media and entertainment app will cost approximately $80,000 - 85,000. This cost includes services of managers, developers, designers and QA engineers.

  • What are 5 common problems in the software development process?

    1. Unclear requirements Right requirements are the base of each software development process. Unclear requirements also wrong estimation of time and budget. 2. Poor communication Miscommunication can lead to missed deadlines and can incur additional expenses. 3. Software bugs Undervaluing QA testing is the biggest issue here. Software development and software testing should go arm in arm to make the development process more purposeful. 4. Time estimation It’s the crucial point of project evaluation. You need to estimate each task and give it time frames but also give a buffer. 5. Implementation Software development process works when everything is implemented step-by-step. Choose a software development team who knows how to implement things right.

  • What is the purpose of entertainment software?

    Entertainment software is a software that provides forms of amusement. But some forms of such software can also be informative or educational while being entertaining.
